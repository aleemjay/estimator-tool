# Pricing interview (Phase 0)

Goal: turn AJ's in-his-head pricing into `pricing/rules.yaml` that the quote
engine can apply. Work through this in a session; capture answers inline, then
encode. Validate the encoded rules by re-pricing 3–5 past real jobs and
comparing to what was actually quoted.

## 1. Coating systems and base rates

For each system you quote, the base $/sq ft and how it breaks by size:

- Epoxy flake (full broadcast): rate? size breakpoints (e.g. <500, 500–2k, 2k–10k, 10k+)?
- Quartz (double broadcast): rate + breakpoints?
- Urethane cement (kitchens, wet areas): rate + breakpoints?
- Solid-color epoxy / high-build: rate + breakpoints?
- Polished concrete (by gloss level / grit spec): rate + breakpoints?
- Sealed concrete / densifier: rate + breakpoints?
- Fluid-applied flooring (e.g. the Fort Myers fleet-shop type invite): rate?
- Anything else you bid (cove base per LF? line striping? joint fill per LF?)

## 2. Prep and substrate

- Standard grind/prep: included in base rate or separate line?
- Shot blast vs diamond grind — when, and price difference?
- Moisture mitigation (vapor barrier epoxy): $/sq ft, and when do you assume
  it's needed vs exclude-with-note?
- Crack/joint repair: how priced (per LF, allowance, T&M)?
- Existing coating removal: how priced?

## 3. Job-level adders and minimums

- Minimum job price (would you mobilize for under $X?)
- Mobilization / travel: flat? by distance from base (Orlando area?)?
  Fort Myers / Bonita Springs jobs are 3+ hours out — surcharge?
- Night/weekend work (occupied sites like vet clinics, restaurants)?
- Multiple mobilizations (phased construction schedules)?
- GC-specific factors: any GCs you price higher/lower (payment history,
  retainage, bid-shopping)?

## 4. Margins and strategy

- Target margin overall? Different by system or job size?
- "Want it" vs "don't want it" pricing — what makes a job attractive
  (location, size, GC, schedule)?
- Rounding rules for the final number?

## 5. Exclusions and assumptions (boilerplate)

- Standard exclusions list on every quote (e.g. moisture mitigation unless
  tested, furniture moving, after-hours, bonds)?
- Standard validity period, payment terms, warranty language?
- Pull these from past Joist quotes if easier.
